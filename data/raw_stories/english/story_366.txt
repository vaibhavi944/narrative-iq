Once upon a time, there was a parade. Everyone was wearing comfortable clothes and were happy. They all had something to attach to their outfit. Some had balloons, others had ribbons, and some had flags. Everyone was having a great time.

The parade slowly made its way through the town. Everyone was smiling and waving, and the parade music was very loud and happy. Everyone felt so comfortable and excited.

The parade finally ended, and everyone cheered. They all said goodbye to their friends, hugged each other, and thanked the parade leader for organizing such a wonderful event. Everyone was so comfortable and content.

They all went away feeling very happy, knowing that they had been part of a great parade. And from now on, each time they saw a parade, they would remember how wonderful and comfortable it was.
