Once upon a time there was a little girl who lived in a big world. She spoke and smiled at everyone she saw.

But the other people in the world didn't talk to her. They only talked to each other. The little girl felt very spoiled.

One day, the little girl was walking along a path. Suddenly, she heard a small voice whisper, "Hello". It was coming from a small, furry creature.

The little girl was very surprised. She began to talk to the creature, who was a special fairy. The fairy said that the little girl had a very kind heart.

The little girl was so happy that someone had finally spoken to her. She thanked the fairy, who waved goodbye and flew away.

From then on, the little girl felt much less spoiled. Whenever she felt lonely, she remembered her special friend and smiled.
