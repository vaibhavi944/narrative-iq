The little boy lived in a quiet house. He loved to play outside. One day he decided to serve his family. He gathered some special things in a basket. He made some food and put it in the basket. He was very proud of his work.

The little boy brought the basket to his parents. He said it was a surprise. His parents were so happy and said he was very useful. They thanked him and gave him a big hug.

The next day, the little boy saw a stupid cat outside. He wanted to serve the cat some food, so he put some food in a bowl and put it outside. The cat looked very happy with his surprise and he purred quietly. The little boy was so happy he had been useful.
