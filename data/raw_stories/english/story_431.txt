Jimmy was walking through the park when suddenly he saw a beautiful rainbow. He had never seen one before. But then he noticed someone was frowning at the rainbow. Jimmy wondered why they didn't look happy.

He asked the person why they were so unhappy. The person said they were ignorant and did not understand how beautiful the rainbow was. Jimmy smiled. He knew the person needed help understanding the beauty of a rainbow.

He taught the person about rainbows, explaining that all the colors came from the water in the sky. The person started to smile and thanked Jimmy for his help.

Jimmy was so happy he had changed someone's frown into a smile by teaching them about rainbows. He knew that everyone should be able to enjoy the beauty of a rainbow, no matter how ignorant they might be.
