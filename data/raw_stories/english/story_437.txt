At first, it was sunny outside. Then, the sky became serious and it started to rain. 

Mama and Lily were very sad. They were hoping to go outside and play. But it was too wet and rainy to do that now.

Mama had an idea. She said, "Let's make a special treat inside! We can be serious and do our best."

So that's what they did. They worked together to make a delicious cake. Lily started mixing the ingredients, while Mama put it in the oven. 

When it was done baking, the cake smelled and looked so good! They had fun eating it and even shared some with their neighbors. 

Even though it was raining outside, Lily and Mama had a happy time together inside.
