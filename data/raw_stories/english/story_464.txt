The little boy noticed a furry pig walking around his backyard. It was so small and cute. He watched with glee as the pig seemed to be looking for something. Then, the pig suddenly stopped and dug a big hole in the ground. He watched as the pig put something in the hole and then covered it with dirt. 

The pig sat on the dirt for a few moments and then the little boy noticed that it was having a snack. It was eating the soft and juicy green grass. After a few bites, the little pig hopped away to find some more food. 

The little boy was so excited to know that he shared his backyard with this furry pig. He decided that he would come back the next day to see if the pig was still there.
