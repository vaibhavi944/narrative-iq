One day, a little girl named Emma went to the park with her dad. She was so excited! As soon as she arrived, she looked around and saw a tall stand filled with delicious treats. Emma's dad told her she could pick one. She took her time, carefully observing all the options. Then, she chose a big, red lollipop. It looked so delicious! She enjoyed it as they watched the ducks swimming in the pond. It was a lovely day spent in the park.
