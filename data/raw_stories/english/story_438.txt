Once upon a time there was a boy who liked to wander in the woods.
One day he set off, curious and excited to explore the unknown. As he looked around, the boy saw many harmless creatures, all enjoying their day in the sun. 
The boy was careful and stepped lightly, looking around him the whole time.
He stumbled across a meadow with the prettiest flowers he had ever seen. He could have stayed there all day. 
But soon, the wind started to blow and the boy decided it was time to go. He bid farewell to the birds and the butterflies and gracefully wandered back to his home.
