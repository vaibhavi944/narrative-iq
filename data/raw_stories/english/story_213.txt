Once upon a time there was a girl called Molly. She was three years old and loved learning new things. One day she saw something new, she did not know what it was called. It was a big green thing. She asked her mother, "What is that thing?" Her mother smiled and said: "It's called a plant. Would you like to learn about it?"

Molly was very excited and said: "Yes, please!" Her mother introduced her to the plant and explained how to take care of it. She said: "You need to water it every day, so it will stay healthy". Molly really wanted to make her new plant happy.

Every day, she would water the plant and scream out happiness whenever she saw it grow. She was so happy when she saw the plant grow bigger and bigger. She was very persistent in taking care of the plant, and she never stopped. Finally, the plant blossomed and Molly was so proud. From then on, Molly introduced her plant to everyone she knew.
