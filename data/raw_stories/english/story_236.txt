Once upon a time, there was a brave bear called Bob. Every day, Bob would take his friend, a little rabbit, with him on all his adventures.

One day, Bob saw an interesting zigzag path in the forest. He urgently wanted to explore it, so he urged his friend to join him.

"Let's go, Rabbit! It's gonna be an exciting journey!" he exclaimed.

But Rabbit was feeling a bit afraid. "It's very dry and there are so many zigzags," he said nervously.

"Don't worry," said Bob reassuringly, "I'm here with you. Nothing bad will happen."

Rabbit felt a bit better. With a happy heart, he followed Bob onto the zigzag path. Together, they explored the mysterious path and had a great adventure!
