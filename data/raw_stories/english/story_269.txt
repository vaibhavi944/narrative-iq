Once upon a time, there was a little girl called Claire. She was very brave and powerful. One day, she was playing in her yard when she saw a patient sitting on the grass. The patient was crying and seemed very sad. She went over to the patient and asked, "What's wrong?"

The patient told Claire that she had fallen from her bed earlier that day. Claire was determined to help the patient, so she put her powerful hands on the patient's back and tried to lift them off the ground. But the patient was too heavy.

Claire thought and thought. She decided that the only way she could help the patient was to get help from her mom and dad. She ran into her home and called her parents. Then they all helped the patient back onto her bed.

The patient thanked Claire and said, "You are a very brave and powerful girl!" Claire smiled. She was happy she was able to help.
