Once upon a time, in a small village there lived a young girl called Lucy. She was very popular among her friends for her vibrant and happy personality. One day, when Lucy was walking past the park, she spotted something in the grass. When she looked closer, she saw it was an old purse! 

Lucy was so excited and she bent down to pick it up. When she opened the purse, she found lots of coins inside. She was so happy that she could now buy herself some new toys. 

But when she tried to stand up again, Lucy suddenly realised that she was stuck. She had been using her purse to balance herself and in the process had become tangled up in the long grass. 

Lucy started to struggle to try and get untangled, but the long grass was too powerful. She began to cry and shout for help. 

Just then, one of her friends walking past the park heard her cries and came to her rescue. 

Her friend put down his popular baseball cap and helped Lucy free herself. After a few minutes of struggling, Lucy was free. She thanked her friend and gave him a big hug. 

The two of them stopped to count the coins inside the purse before walking back to Lucy's house. 

The End!
