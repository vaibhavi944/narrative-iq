Once upon a time, there was a delicate boat floating in the ocean. It was getting tired so it decided to take a rest. The boat gently floated to a beautiful harbor. The harbor was full of many boats like the delicate one. The delicate boat was happy to be in the harbor. All the boats rested in the harbor together. There was a nice breeze blowing. Everyone was happy and safe in the harbor. The delicate boat went to sleep in the harbor and felt cozy and content. 

The boats stayed in the harbor for a long time to get some rest. After a while, the boats started to wake up and were ready to sail again. The delicate boat was happy to be in the harbor and thanked them all for the peace it had found. 

The delicate boat was so happy that it had found a place to rest in the harbor. It said goodbye to the other boats and started sailing again. The delicate boat went on many adventures, but it would always remember the peaceful moments it had spent in the harbor.
