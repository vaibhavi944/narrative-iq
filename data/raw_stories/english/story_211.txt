Once upon a time, there lived a little boy named Jimmy. One cold day, Jimmy was playing with a knife in his yard. He thought it was fun to use the knife to cut things. 

Out of the corner of his eye, Jimmy saw his dad, who said to him, "Jimmy, knives are not toys. You must be very careful with them, or you could get hurt!"

Jimmy was scared and put the knife away. From then on, he was more careful whenever he was holding a knife.

The moral of the story is that we should always increase our care and caution when using sharp objects like knives.
