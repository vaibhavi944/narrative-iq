Mary and James were happily playing together in the summer sunshine. Mary thought to herself that summer was very attractive. She smiled, then said to James, "Let's add something to our game."

"What should we add?" asked James.

"What about if we pretend there is a cave under the tree, and we have to explore it. We can take a stick and a pretend map!" Mary replied.

James's eyes lit up with excitement. "That sounds great!"

Mary and James gathered some sticks and stones, and began their adventures. They pretended they were discovering all kinds of things in their pretend cave, and enjoyed their game so much they didn't want to stop.

The two friends made up amazing stories as they explored, adding new ideas to their game. Summer was the best time to adventure together and have lots of fun!
