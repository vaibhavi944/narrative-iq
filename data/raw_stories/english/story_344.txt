Molly and her mom were sitting outside in the sun. It was Wednesday and they were enjoying their healthy snack.

"Look what I made," said mom.

Molly looked over and saw Mom had made a picture using a crayon and some markers.

"That's so cool!" said Molly. 

"Do you want to help me mark the rest of it?" asked Mom.

Molly nodded and they continued to mark the picture together. Molly asked all sorts of questions while they worked. 

When they were done, the colorful picture was complete. 

"Mom, can we do this again next Wednesday?" asked Molly with a smile.

Mom laughed and said, "Of course, sweetheart."
