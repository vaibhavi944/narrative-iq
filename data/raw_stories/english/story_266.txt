Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Molly. Molly had a great big, hairy dog. They were very happy together.

One day, Molly and her dog were out walking in the park when they heard a noise. Molly was scared and she hugged her dog tight. Then something strange happened. A ghost appeared beneath a tree. It was a very strange sight. 

Molly's dog growled at the ghost. The ghost ran away and Molly felt brave. 

"What was that, Molly?" asked her dog. 

"I don't know," replied Molly. 

As they continued their walk, Molly noticed something strange. Whenever Molly said the word "yield", ghosts appeared from the trees! She had the magic power to make ghosts yield.

Molly was excited and scared at the same time. She decided to keep this magic to herself. Molly went home and hugged her hairy dog. She knew she was safe.
