Once upon a time, there was a boy who had a round, soft rug. He loved to play on it. One day he wanted to do something different. He decided to number the rug. So, the boy got his markers and carefully he started to number each side of the rug. The boy was slow and precise. He must make sure that the numbers were neat and even. Finally, the number rug was complete and the boy was very happy. Every day he wakes up, jumps onto the rug and counts the numbers. It was so much fun to sit and relax on his special number rug!
