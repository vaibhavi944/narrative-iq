Once upon a time, there was a nosy little cat called Fluffy. One day, Fluffy was wandering around when she noticed something shiny. It was a pattern of lines on the ground. She had never seen anything like it before and couldn't help but have a closer look. She started to stir the lines with her paw.

When she stirred, something magical happened. Suddenly, the lines began to move around and turn into shapes and colours. Fluffy couldn't believe her eyes!

Soon, the shapes and colours began to move. They started to form a beautiful dance and soon a story emerged. Fluffy watched in wonder as the shapes danced around and told their magical story.

When the story was over, Fluffy knew she had to find out what the pattern was. She went home and asked everyone she knew, but no one could answer her.

Until one day, her nosy friend George found out about the magical pattern. He said it was called a mandala. Fluffy was so excited to learn about her new discovery and couldn't wait to show it to her friends!
