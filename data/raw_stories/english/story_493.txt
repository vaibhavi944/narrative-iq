Once upon a time there was a little girl. Her name was Millie. She was 3-years-old and very curious. One day, Millie sneaked away and went to explore a nearby bush. Millie forgot about the time and soon her mom called her back home. Millie was embarrassed because she had been gone so long. 

When Millie got home, her mom decided to cook them a special dinner. She made Millie a yummy bowl of spaghetti and meatballs. Millie ate every bite and was so happy. 

Millie never ever snuck away to explore another bush again. She was just too embarrassed. 

The end.
