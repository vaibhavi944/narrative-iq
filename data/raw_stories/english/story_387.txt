Once upon a time, there lived a bald figure in the forest. He was called the Acceptor. Everyone in the forest respected him very much because he would accept anything that was given to him with a kind and friendly heart.

Once a week, all the animals in the forest would give him special presents. The Acceptor would accept them all with a big smile. He was so kind and happy to accept every gift that was given to him.

One day, a little 3 year old girl named Mary entered the forest. Mary saw the Acceptor and was so surprised to see how friendly and bald he was. She waved at him and showed him her present that she had brought for him. The Acceptor was so happy to accept it with a big smile on his face. 

From then on, the Acceptor and Mary became best friends. Every week, Mary would bring the Acceptor a special present. The Acceptor was happy to accept them all and thanked Mary very much.

And that is why the forest was always full of peace and joy, thanks to the Acceptor's kind heart.
