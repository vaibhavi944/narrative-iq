Once upon a time there was a very wealthy family who owned many things, but the family's most cherished possession was the puppy that the father had bought for the children that same day. The puppy was small and fluffy with big, black eyes. 

The family went outside to play with the puppy, and they laughed with joy as they watched the puppy chase around butterflies. Suddenly, the puppy started to shiver with cold and the family looked up to see storm clouds gathering in the sky. 

The mother said to the father, "Let's go inside quick and get the pup warm." But the father said, "It's ok, the pup will be fine in this weather," and he began to walk away.

The children were about to shiver too, but the mother quickly picked up the puppy and rushed everyone inside to safety.

The family was so thankful that the mother had acted quickly and that the puppy was safe. The mom said to the dad, "See, that's why I always listen to my instincts." The family smiled and hugged their beloved puppy tight, thanking the mom for her wisdom.
