One day, a little boy was playing in his garden. He was excited to feel the sun on his face and the grass beneath his feet. Suddenly, he noticed a spider crawling towards him. He became scared and started to move away.

The spider started chasing the little boy, who ran back and forth, looking for a place to hide. He was frustrated that the spider kept following him. Just then, the boy's mother appeared. She said, "What is going on here?"

The little boy replied, "Mummy, there's a spider chasing me. I don't like it."

His mother smiled and said, "That's okay. Spiders won't hurt you. Just try and stay calm and relax."

The little boy tried to take a deep breath and slowly, he began to feel less scared. He said, "Okay, I'm not scared anymore." The spider soon stopped chasing him and he was no longer frustrated. He smiled and said, "Thank you, Mummy!"
