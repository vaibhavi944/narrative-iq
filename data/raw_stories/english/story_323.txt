Once upon a time there was a poor witch. One day she was walking through the woods when she heard a loud sound. She looked up and saw an arrow shooting through the sky. She caught the arrow and saw that it was from a little boy with a bow and arrow.

The witch said: "Hello, little boy! Why are you shooting arrows?"

The boy said: "I'm trying to hit apples off the tree so I can eat them."

The witch said: "That's a very brave thing to do. Do you need any help?"

The boy said: "Yes, please! I could use some help."

So the witch used her magic to make a ladder appear so the little boy could climb up to get his apples. The little boy was so happy, he gave the witch a big hug and thanked her for being so helpful. 

The witch smiled and said: "You're welcome, little one. Always remember to help those who are poor like me." 

The boy thanked the witch again and the witch flew away on her broomstick. The little boy was very happy and he thanked the witch for all her help.
