Once upon a time, there was a pig who wanted to escape his pen. He tried pushing the gate open, but it was too hard. He asked the farmer, "Can you fix it?" The farmer said, "No, that gate is too strong. You cannot escape." The pig was sad. He felt so dirty in his pen, and he wanted to be free. Suddenly, the pig had an idea. He went to find some tools and started to work on the gate. After a while, he managed to fix the gate, and set himself free! The pig was so happy he could escape his dirty pen. He raced off through the grass and the flowers and never looked back. From then on, he lived happily ever after.
