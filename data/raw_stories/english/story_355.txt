Once there was a brave jeep. He was an old jeep and had been around for many years. One day he decided he wanted to have an adventure! He drove off into the night. 

Everywhere he looked, he could see animals and stars in the sky. After a while he came across a big hill. He wanted to climb the hill but it was too steep. He tried and tried but he couldn't make it to the top. 

Suddenly, he heard a loud noise. It was a group of brave soldiers in a jeep coming up the hill! The jeep was very strong and brave. He knew he would never be able to beat the jeep. So, he decided to surrender and let them drive up the hill.

The jeep and the soldiers climbed to the top of the hill. The jeep was very proud. He was no match for the brave jeep and the soldiers. He had surrendered but he learned what it meant to be brave.
