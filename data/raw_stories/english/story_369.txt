Once upon a time there was a charming little girl and her father. Every day they liked to play together. One day the little girl had a paper in her hand. She ran to her dad, teasing him. Her dad smiled and said 'let's find something fun to do with the paper'. The little girl agreed.

They thought and thought. Finally, the dad had a fun idea. He taught the little girl how to make different shapes with the paper. She laughed and smiled as she twisted and folded the paper together.

Next, the dad showed her how to make a pretty paper flower. She was so happy that she ran around the house with it in her hand. As she ran she yelled 'Look at my charming flower, daddy!'. Her dad was so proud.

The dad and the little girl had a wonderful time playing together and learning about paper. And from that day on, they made sure to have lots of paper, so they could have fun and make things together.
