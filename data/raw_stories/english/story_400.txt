Mummy and Daddy took baby to have a bath. Baby smiled and splashed. Mummy warned baby that the bath water was dangerous and to be careful. Baby nodded, understanding mummy's warning. 

Then Daddy put bubbles in the water and baby was so excited! As the bubbles filled the tub, baby started to laugh. Mummy and Daddy watched, laughing too. 

Mummy explained again that the water was dangerous, but Daddy helped baby to stand up and the fun began! Baby squealed with joy as the bubbles and the warm water tickled their skin. Daddy told baby to splash and jump, and baby happily did. 

Finally, Mummy and Daddy decided it was time for baby to come out. Baby pouted, but Mummy said it was okay. They all got out of the tub, and Mummy wrapped baby in a big, warm towel. Baby felt safe and happy, and they all headed off to bed.
