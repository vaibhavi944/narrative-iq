The sun shone brightly in the sky, making the grass very dry.

Tommy and his dad were outside playing football. Tommy enjoyed playing football with his dad very much.

Suddenly, Tommy started to sneeze. He sneezed so hard that he dropped the football.

His dad said, "Bless you, Tommy. Take a break and have a drink of water."

Tommy sat down and drank his water. After a few sips, he felt much better.

He said to his dad, "Can we play some more football now? It's a lot of fun!"

His dad smiled and said, "Yes, let's go!"

They started playing football again and had a great time. Tommy never wanted the day to end.
