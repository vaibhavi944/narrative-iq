Once there was a tall boy. He was trying to decide what he should do. He thought and thought but he just couldn't make up his mind.

Suddenly he had an idea. He decided to get his knife. With the knife he knew he could do many things. He figured solving his problem would be easy.

So he took the knife and went outside. He started cutting a tree with it. After a little bit, he had enough wood to build something. He made a tall house on the ground.

He was very pleased with his work. He had decided what he wanted to do and he had made it happen. It was his special tall house and he was proud.
