Once upon a time there was a girl called Sarah. She loved to imagine things. One day she imagined that she was standing in front of a big plate of spaghetti. She imagined the spaghetti was long and yummy. It was the best spaghetti ever!

Sarah loved the taste of spaghetti. She wanted to eat it, so she grabbed a fork and started eating. It was so delicious that she ate it all. She was full and happy.

But then she started feeling competitive. She wanted to know if she could eat even more spaghetti! So she imagined a big bowl of spaghetti was in front of her. This time it was even bigger than before! It was a big challenge for Sarah.

Sarah had to be brave and eat it all. But she was brave and she ate the whole bowl of spaghetti! She was so proud of herself. She had managed to eat the biggest bowl of spaghetti!
