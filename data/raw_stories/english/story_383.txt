Once upon a time, there was a little girl who wanted to observe what was going on near her. She saw a thermometer and thought it was very interesting. She got closer to get a much better look.

Suddenly a rude man appeared and began to shout at her. The little girl was very scared and started to cry. She ran away quickly. 

The little girl was sad, because she couldn't observe the thermometer anymore. She wanted to know more about it and find out why the rude man didn't want her to look at it.

The little girl decided that one day she would go back to observe the thermometer and find out why the rude man had been so mean. She was determined to get the answer!
