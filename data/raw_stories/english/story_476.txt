Once upon a time there was a guard. He was very nervous because he had to stay awake all night. He was watching over something special. He was very careful and he watched all night. 

When morning came the guard was very tired. He had been so busy watching that he hadn't slept at all. He needed to sleep. So he found a nice, comfortable spot and dozed off. 

But soon the guard was being watched. A sneaky fox had seen the guard asleep and was getting closer and closer. The fox was looking for something special that the guard was protecting.
 
The guard woke up and was very scared. He was no longer nervous, he was very scared. He saw the fox and he jumped up. He made lots of noise and waved his arms. The fox was so scared that it ran away!

The guard was very happy. He was sure he had saved something special. He knew that he had to stay awake so he stayed on guard for the rest of the night. From then on he was never so nervous and he made sure he slept enough.
