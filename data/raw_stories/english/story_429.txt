One day, 2 friends were playing near a line. The line was very long and tough. The friends were smiling and having fun. Suddenly, they saw a large gust of wind. The gust of wind was so strong that it separated the line! The friends had never seen anything like it before.

The friends looked around trying to figure out what to do. Then, they realized that they needed to put the line back together. They worked together and pulled really hard. It took them a long time, but eventually they got the line back to the way it was before.

The friends smiled at each other and hugged. They were so proud of themselves. They had worked together and shown that even tough lines can be separated and put back together.
