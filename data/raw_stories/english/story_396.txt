Mommy was very excited and she said, "Let's unpack!"
Mommy took out some new clothes from the box. First, she took out a new skirt. It was pink and had pretty flowers on it.
Mommy put on the skirt and said, "How do I look?" Her friend said, "You look great!"
But then a naughty boy came up and said, "That skirt looks ugly." Mommy looked very angry and said, "That was very rude!"
Mommy put her hands on her hips and said, "It's time for you to leave!" The naughty boy ran away and Mommy said, "Let's finish unpacking!"
They unpacked some more clothes and they were all happy.
