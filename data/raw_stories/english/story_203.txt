Once upon a time there was a heavy truck. The truck belonged to a man called Ben. One day, the truck stopped working, so Ben took it to a mechanic.

When Ben arrived at the mechanic's shop, the mechanic told him that the truck had frozen. He said it needed fixing before Ben could drive it away.

Ben asked the mechanic, "Can you fix it?"

The mechanic looked at the truck and said, "Yes, I will fix it. But it will take me some time."

So Ben left the truck at the mechanic's shop and came back a few days later.

When he arrived, the mechanic said, "It is fixed! You can drive your truck away now."

Ben was so happy. He drove the truck home, and it never froze again.
