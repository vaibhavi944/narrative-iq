Once upon a time there was an orange leak. The leak was always getting bigger and bigger. Until one day it split. The orange leak split into two. Each part of the orange leak was still leaking and getting bigger. Soon the two orange leaks had spread across the floor. But then something surprising happened. A big sunny ray of light came down from the sky and filled the two orange leaks with sunshine. The orange leaks smiled and started to jump and dance in the sunshine. From that day on, the orange leaks never split again and always stayed together.
