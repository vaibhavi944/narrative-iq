Once upon a time, there was a girl called Lucy. She was very small, only three years old, and sometimes felt helpless. Lucy wanted to explore the world, so one day she decided to wander. 

She went far from home and eventually stumbled upon a weird stage. On the stage was an old man who saw Lucy and asked her if she wanted to join him. She nodded, filled with excitement about what she would see. 

The old man put on a show, but Lucy didn't understand one bit of it. She became frustrated and started to cry and told the old man that she wanted to go home. He laughed and continued the show. 

Lucy felt helpless and didn't know what to do. Suddenly, a woman walked on to the stage and said to the old man, "Let the little girl go. She's too young to understand what you are doing." 

The old man gave a funny smile and nodded. He said to Lucy, "You can go home now - remember never to wander too far again." Lucy smiled and wandered back home, feeling a little wiser.
