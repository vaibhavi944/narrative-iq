One day, a cheerful little girl named Molly went to the park. Everywhere around her were exciting things to play with and discover.

Molly heard some clapping and went to investigate. To her delight, she found a large group of people clapping, singing, and dancing all around her.

Molly decided to join in the fun. She clapped above her head, danced around the park, and sang along to the music. Soon, everyone was singing, clapping and dancing together.

Then a carrot appeared, making its way around the circle. Everyone was so excited and clapped even louder.

Molly was so happy with the surprise that she smiled and clapped her hands again. All of a sudden, the carrot was opened up and a big bunch of colorful balloons were released in the sky.

The sky was so cheerful and beautiful with all the colorful balloons, everyone was clapping and laughing. Molly was so excited and started clapping with joy.

The cheerful day was a very special one for Molly and she was sure she never would forget it.
