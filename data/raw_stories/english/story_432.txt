Once upon a time, there was a little boy who found a shiny coin while walking in the park. He was so excited to have found the coin that he ran home to tell his mum. He showed her the coin and it made her smile. 

The little boy thought he would use the coin to buy something special, so he put it in his pocket. He decided to go back to the park to surprise his mum with something special.

In the park, he found a harsh-looking man selling bright, colourful balloons. He thought they would be the perfect surprise for his mum. He bought the balloons with his coin and took them home. 

His mum was so surprised when she saw the balloons that she was speechless. The little boy had found the perfect surprise for his mum and the coin had made it all possible.
