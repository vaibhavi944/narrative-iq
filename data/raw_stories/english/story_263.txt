One sunny day, a pirate was wandering through the woods. He had a big, shining hat, two swords and a wooden leg. Suddenly he heard a noise coming from the bushes. The pirate stopped and listened carefully.

"Ouch, it hurts", a small voice called out from the bushes.

The pirate went to investigate, and he saw a little girl, who was crying. The pirate asked her why she was crying, but she only shook her head.

The pirate lifted the little girl out of the bushes, and asked her again why she was so hurt. This time she told the pirate that she had been playing hide-and-seek with her friend, and had gotten lost.

The pirate put his arm around the little girl, and smiled down at her. He promised he would lead her home, so she wouldn't be hurt anymore. As the two walked, the pirate told her stories about his adventures of sailing the seven seas.

The pirate took the little girl home, and stayed to make sure she was safe. Both the little girl and the pirate were happy about this. The little girl had learned an important lesson - always listen to your parents, so you don't get lost. And the pirate had learned a lesson too - helping out others can make you feel good.
