Once there was a brother who was very cheerful. He loved to laugh and play. One day he wanted to cover himself up so he got his blanket. He spread it out over his head and body and quickly it covered him up. Now he couldn't see anything and it made him laugh. He was having so much fun.

Then his brother saw what he was doing and decided to join in. He got his own blanket and covered himself up too. Now the two of them were both covered up with their blankets. They laughed and giggled as they felt the warmth from the blankets.

After a while, they took off their blankets. The two brothers looked at each other and smiled. The cheerful brother's face was full of joy! They had so much fun being covered up and giggling like little kids.
