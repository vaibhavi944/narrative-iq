One day, Jack went fishing with his dad. Jack loved to go fishing. His dad gave him a gentle fishing rod and told him to stir the water with it. Jack was so excited, he started to stir the water with the rod. As he gently stirred, something mysterious happened. Suddenly, the water was filled with beautiful fish! Jack and his dad couldn't believe it. It was a magic mystery! Jack had so much fun fishing with his dad that day. They caught many fish and had a tasty fish dinner. They were so happy.
