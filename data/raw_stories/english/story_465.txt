Once there was a rare crane that flew in the sky. It was the most wonderful crane anyone had ever seen! It was so beautiful, people came from all around the world just for a chance to glimpse the crane.

One day, a little boy wanted to catch the crane. So he grabbed his net and set off looking for the crane. Everywhere he went, people said the crane had gone, but he kept looking.

Finally, he found it! He was so excited; he jumped up and down. Then he slowly crept forward, net in hand. Before he knew it, the crane was in his net!

The little boy was so proud of himself. He had caught the rare crane all by himself. He could tell everyone, show them what he had done.

From that day on, the little boy never gave up looking for a rare crane again. He always remembered how special it was to catch one.
