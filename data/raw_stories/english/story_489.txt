Once upon a time there was a little octopus. Every day he would bounce around the ocean and have lots of fun. One day, he decided to bounce up higher and higher until he reached the surface of the water. He could now see the world outside the ocean!

The little octopus decided to bounce around the beach. He saw a ball and decided to bounce it back to a little boy. The little boy was so excited and ran away with the ball. The octopus was so proud of himself, he bounced all around the beach in celebration. 

Soon, the little octopus became tired. He hopped back into the water and took a nap. After he woke up, he decided to go explore the ocean deeper down and went off on a new adventure.
