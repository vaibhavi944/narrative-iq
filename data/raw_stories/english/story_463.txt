Jimmy was an really busy boy. Every day he was looking for adventure.
One day, he was outside playing when a strange big man appeared from behind a tree. He was bald, with a big red nose and a shiny nail on his finger. 
Jimmy was surprised and a little scared, but the man smiled and started to talk to him. He said he was a magical man and could make special things appear just by clicking his nail. 
Jimmy was so excited! The man clicked his nail and magcally, a giant ice cream appeared! Jimmy was so happy he ate the whole thing. 
The magical man waved goodbye and then he was gone. Jimmy never saw him again, but he always remembered the special day.
